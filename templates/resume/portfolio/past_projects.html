Adopt Chester

Adopt Chester was a project designed to find a Forever Home for our neighborhood friend, Chester. He came to us with an injured paw, and we agreed to foster him through the Humane Rescue Alliance. We attempted to find his original owner, but were unsuccessful. We became his foster parents and it came time to advertise him, so I decided to put together a quick site to show how pretty and playful he was. Within 1 day, we had 2 responses to our Craigslist post linking to the website, and within a week, Chester had finally found his Forever home and was off to start his new life!

Feel free to visit Adopt Chester Today! if you want to see how pretty he was, or if you're interested in an example of a pet advertisement website.

Oborug Obstacles

Oborug Obstacles was a page that was created to keep track of the Map and Custom Rulings in the "Oborug Obstacles" Dungeons and Dragons campaign, run by me with a group of friends. Although the gaming group ended up disbanding shortly after due to time commitment issues, the site remains as an example of a way to centralize information and assets for distribution to a group or team.

Check out Oborug Obstacles if you want to see the map that we had explored so far, or if you're curious about setting up static information sites.

Whelming Web

Whelming Web is a website for The Whelming West, a new card game being developed. As part of the game design, I have created various tools to assist our process. Whelming Web is a combination of:

  • Flask database website hosted on Heroku for keeping track of card designs and game history;
  • PyQt5 applications for card design, game setup, and game testing; and
  • Asset-generation software for automated creation of cards (including TableTop Simulator compatibility!)

Stay tuned for announcements on public beta testing and development progress!
